AI Music Genre Classifier – CNN Architecture

Input: MFCC (130 x 13 x 1)

Layer 1:
Conv2D (32 filters, 3x3) + ReLU
BatchNormalization
MaxPooling2D (3x3)

Layer 2:
Conv2D (32 filters, 3x3) + ReLU
BatchNormalization
MaxPooling2D (3x3)

Layer 3:
Conv2D (32 filters, 2x2) + ReLU
BatchNormalization
MaxPooling2D (2x2)

Flatten

Dense (64 neurons) + ReLU
Dropout (0.3)

Output Layer:
Dense (10 neurons) + Softmax

Loss Function:
Categorical Crossentropy

Optimizer:
Adam

Evaluation Metrics:
Accuracy
F1 Score
